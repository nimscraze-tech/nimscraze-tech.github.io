Privacy Policy - No-PMO Tracker

Simple Version: We don't collect any data. Everything stays on your device. 100% private.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No tracking pixels
  • No device identifiers

Data Storage

All your data (streak count, milestones, journal entries, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No cloud backups
  • No server uploads
  • Completely offline-capable

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: This app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. You can control ad personalization in your device settings.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data

No other third-party services are used.

Permissions

The app requests minimal permissions:

  • Notifications: To send daily motivation and milestone alerts (optional)
  • Internet: Only for displaying ads (AdMob)

User Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to:

  • Request (no data to request)
  • Delete (already deleted/never collected)
  • Export (all data is on your device)

You own 100% of your data. Uninstalling the app deletes everything permanently.

Children's Privacy

This app is rated Mature 17+ and is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and notify users via an in-app message.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at: support@nopmotracker.com

Bottom Line: Your privacy is absolute. No data collection, no tracking, no exceptions.
